## Description

Hello, this is where I post my solutions in my programming competitions. I am aware that Python may not
be the best programming language for competitive programming due to how slow it is compared to other
languages. I do however, plan on learning a different programming language in the future that may be
beneficial for me both in project making and competitive programming.

## Disclaimer

I only post my solutions here after the competition has ended. It's to abide by the rules to not post
solutions while a competition is still in progess to avoid cheating participants. Specifically in
CodeForces and CodeChef.

The counter in `Misc/Counter` is just a webpage containing a counter to count the amount of
